Our Analysis

DNA Analysis

* DNA tests analyze your unique genetic code to reveal information about your health, ancestry, or biological relationships. These tests can identify predispositions to certain diseases, confirm paternity

* The result of analysis will appear after 5 days

Gland Analysis

* Thyroid tests are blood tests that measure the levels of hormones produced by your thyroid gland, a small butterfly-shaped gland in your neck. The most common tests include Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH), T3, and T4

* The result of analysis will appear after 1 day

Liver Analysis

* Liver function tests (LFTs) are blood tests that measure enzymes and proteins in your blood, providing insights into the health and function of your liver. They help doctors detect liver damage or disease, such as hepatitis or cirrhosis

* The result of analysis will appear after 2 days

Blood Analysis

* Blood tests are common medical procedures that involve taking a sample of your blood, usually from a vein in your arm. These tests help doctors check your overall health and can detect a wide range of conditions, such as infections, anemia, or diabetes.

* The result of analysis will appear after 3 hours

Sugar Analysis

* Blood sugar tests measure the amount of glucose in your blood, which is your body's main source of energy. These tests are crucial for diagnosing and managing conditions like diabetes and prediabetes.

* The result of analysis will appear after 1 hour

Tumor Analysis

* Tumor marker tests are blood tests that look for specific substances (markers) that are often produced by cancer cells or by the body in response to cancer. While high levels can suggest the presence of cancer, they are not used alone for diagnosis

* The result of analysis will appear after 6 days

Virus Analysis

Virus tests are crucial diagnostic tools that identify the presence of viral infections in the body . These tests often look for viral genetic material (like DNA or RNA) or antibodies produced by your immune system in response to a virus.

* The result of analysis will appear after 12 hours

How to Book a Lab Test at a Hospital Laboratory ?

1-) Get a Doctor's Referral : Obtain a written referral or prescription from your doctor detailing the specific lab tests you need. This is usually a mandatory requirement.

2-) Contact the Lab for an Appointment : Call the hospital's laboratory department or visit their website to inquire about booking procedures and schedule an appointment. Confirm if fasting or any other special preparations are needed.

3-) Attend Your Appointment with Documents : Arrive at the lab at your scheduled time. Make sure to bring your doctor's referral, identification, and any relevant insurance information
