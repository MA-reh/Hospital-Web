Here You Can Order Any Medicine Online

* Home Delivery Service :
* We offer a convenient home delivery service for your medications.
* This service is available for all patients, including those who have been discharged from the hospital.
* Our team ensures that your medications are delivered safely and promptly to your doorstep.
* Delivery is available within Cairo & Giza and may be subject to a nominal delivery fee.
* Please allow 2 Hours for delivery after your prescription is verified.
* How to Order Your Medications Online :
* Request Your Delivery :
* Online: Fill out our simple online delivery request form ######.
* By Phone: Call our pharmacy at (+20) 1040424647 during business hours.
* In-person: Inform the pharmacy staff at the hospital counter that you prefer home delivery .
* Prescription Verification :
* Our pharmacy team will verify your prescription with your doctor.
* You will receive a confirmation message once your prescription is ready for delivery.
* You can pay securely online or arrange payment upon delivery (if applicable).
