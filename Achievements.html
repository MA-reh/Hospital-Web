OUR ACHIEVEMENT

Milestones Since 1990 Since its establishment in 1990, MENG has been dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare and advancing medical excellence. Over the past decades, we've achieved numerous milestones that reflect our commitment to our community and our patients.

* Pioneering Medical Procedures: We were among the first in the region to introduce advanced neurosurgery techniques in 2005, significantly improving patient recovery times and outcomes.

* Expansion of Specialized Departments: In 2000s, we established state-of-the-art departments such as our dedicated Oncology Center, Women's Health Institut, offering comprehensive and specialized care tailored to diverse patient needs.

* Accreditation and Quality Recognition: We proudly achieved a national quality award in 2010, consistently demonstrating our adherence to the highest international standards of patient safetyand quality.

* Community Health Initiatives: We launched impactful community programs, including diabetes awareness campaigns since 1995, reaching thousands of individuals and promoting preventive health.

* Significant Research Contributions: Our commitment to advancing medicine is evident in our involvement in clinical trials for new cancer therapies, contributing to global medical knowledge and better treatments.

* Adoption of Cutting-Edge Technology: We continuously invest in the latest medical technology, such as advanced MRI and CT scanning equipment, ensuring our patients receive the most accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.

* Exceptional Patient Outcomes: Our dedication to patient-centered care is reflected in our consistently high patient satisfaction scores and positive outcomes across various specialties, underscoring our commitment to healing and well-being.
