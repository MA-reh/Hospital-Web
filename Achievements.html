Our Hospital Achievements

MENG Hospital has achieved numerous milestones in healthcare excellence, patient care, and community service. Here are some of our key accomplishments:

Successful Surgeries

1
  • over 5,000 surgeries performed with a 98.7% success rate across various specialties.
  • adoption of minimally invasive and robotic-assisted techniques for faster recovery.
  • 24/7 on-call surgical teams ready to handle critical cases within minutes.
  • Strict sterilization protocols led to zero post-operative infections in 3 consecutive years.
  • Highly specialized units including cardiothoracic, neuro, and orthopedicsurgery.

Patients Treated

1
  • Treated more than 100,000 patients with personalized care plans and follow-up programs.
  • Achieved a 95% patient satisfaction score in the latest national healthcare audit.
  • Reduced average waiting time to less than 10 minutes using a smart queue system.
  • Launched telemedicine services serving remote villages and international patients.
  • Integrated emotional support and wellness coaching into patient recovery programs.

Community Outreach

1
  • Execution of more than 25 free medical convoys to serve remote areas and villages.
  • Providing healthcare to over 10,000 beneficiaries annually outside the hospital.
  • Regular awareness programs on public health and disease prevention.
  • Free cancer, diabetes, and blood pressure screening initiatives for citizens.
  • Partnerships with charitable organizations and schools to support school health and vaccinations.

National Awards

1
  • Voted “Best National Hospital” three years in a row by the Ministry of Health.
  • Recognized for “Excellence in Patient-Centered Care” at the 2024 Health Summit.
  • Received Green Health Award for implementing sustainable hospital operations.
  • Honored for innovations in AI-based diagnostics and smart medical devices.
  • Partnered with top universities for medical research and training programs.
