Health Awareness

Stay informed, stay healthy!

Warnings & What to Avoid

  • Do not touch medical equipment or chemicals without permission.
  • Avoid entering restricted medical zones or operating rooms.
  • Never take any medication without a doctor’s supervision.

In Case of Burns, Injuries, or Infections

  • Minor Burns: Cool the area under running water for 10 minutes. Do not apply ice directly.
  • Cuts or Wounds: Clean with water, cover with a sterile bandage, and seek medical help.
  • Signs of Infection: Redness, swelling, or heat — contact medical staff immediately.

How to Stay Healthy

  • Drink 6 to 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Get 6–8 hours of sleep every night.
  • Walk or exercise for at least 30 minutes per day.

Preventing Diseases

  • Wash hands regularly with soap and water.
  • Use hand sanitizer when water is not available.
  • Wear a mask in crowded places or when showing symptoms.
  • Follow recommended vaccination schedules.
